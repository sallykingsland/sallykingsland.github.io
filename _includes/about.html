About Sally

My name is Sally Kingsland and I am a Jungian psychotherapist (learn more about what that means here). I have also undertaken training with the Australian & NZ Association of Psychotherapists.

I am available to see people in person in inner North Canberra, Australia, or remote via phone or online video-conferencing. I offer online sessions via Coviu, a secure platform that does not require you to download any software.

I work across multiple time zones, including the USA.
