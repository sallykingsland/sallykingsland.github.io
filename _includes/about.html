About Sally

My name is Sally Kingsland, a Jungian psychotherapist. I have also undertaken training with the Australian & NZ Association of Psychotherapists.

I am available to see people in person in inner North Canberra, Australia, or via online video-conferencing. I offer online sessions via Coviu, a secure platform that does not require you to download any software.

I work across multiple time zones, including the USA where I lived for five years.
