About Sally

My name is Sally Kingsland and I am honoured to accompany people, as a spiritual companion, on their journey through life.

These days I work primarily as a Jungian psychotherapist. I have undertaken training with the Australian & NZ Association of Psychotherapists. Therapy tends to involve more regular sessions than spiritual direction which typically occurs about once a month. I do not undertake psychotherapy while serving as a spiritual companion and do not bring spiritual matters into my psychotherapy practice unless specifically requested.
