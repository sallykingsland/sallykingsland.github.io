About Sally

Hello! My name is Sally Kingsland and I am honoured to accompany people, as a spiritual companion, on their journey through life.

For the last 15 years I have been intentionally pursuing spiritual, emotional and physical healing on the path towards wholeness. This has included spiritual formation work, non-violent communication training, spiritual nurture training, being mentored in spiritual direction, and developing in my life as a contemplative. For me, a natural outcome of this path has been to share what I have learned about the eternal and support others on their journey.

I am a mother of primary (elementary) school children and love being in nature, reading, cooking and gardening.

I am actively connected to Quaker and Jewish communities. I am a member of Spiritual Directors International and abide by their Guidelines for Ethical Conduct. I am inspired by, and have a growing interest in, Jungian insights and approaches for inner work.
